One-line Captivating Description: A forensic investigation unmasks a "Trojan Horse" virus hiding in archival yak transcriptomes, revealing a hidden parasitic ecosystem.

This project investigates sOTU u145189, a viral sequence initially annotated as originating from bovine muscle tissue, and demonstrates through phylogenetic and structural analysis that it instead represents a virus associated with a cryptic protozoan host. By combining RdRp motif analysis, AlphaFold structural predictions, and ecological context from yak transcriptome datasets, this work illustrates how archival sequencing data can hide complex parasitic interactions.
